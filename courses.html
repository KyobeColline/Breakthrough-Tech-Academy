Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses

What are the payment options available?

We offer multiple payment options including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and installment plans. Students can also apply for scholarships or financial aid.

Are the courses self-paced?

Yes, most of our courses are self-paced, allowing you to learn at your own convenience. However, some courses may have specific schedules for live sessions and assignments.

Do I get a certificate upon completion?

Yes, you'll receive a verified certificate of completion for each course you successfully complete. These certificates can be shared on LinkedIn and other professional platforms.

What kind of support is available?

We provide 24/7 technical support, access to course mentors, and a community forum where you can interact with other students. Live chat support is also available during business hours.
